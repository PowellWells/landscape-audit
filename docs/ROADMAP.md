# Roadmap

## v0.1

- Header-only C++20 audit core.
- Strict descent and neutral-plateau audit.
- Adapter-defined radius-one and radius-two neighborhoods.
- Deterministic threading, JSON certificates, GraphML, checkpoint/resume.
- Max-SAT, graph-coloring, scheduling, and neutral-trap examples.

## Candidate v0.2 work

- A policy concept for lexicographic objectives.
- Chunked enumeration digests and bounded disk-backed visited sets.
- A stable adapter test harness and property-test corpus.
- Tabular export designed for GraphFLA, NetworkX, and data frames.
- An optional `SymmetryOracle` contract reporting exact, approximate, or unavailable canonicalization.
- A separately benchmarked high-performance Erdős #617 adapter.

## Explicit non-goals for the near term

- Becoming a general metaheuristic solver.
- Reimplementing global fitness-landscape statistics.
- Distributed or GPU plateau enumeration.
- A general computational group theory package.
- Automatically composing arbitrary k-move neighborhoods.
